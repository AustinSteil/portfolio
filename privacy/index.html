Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of Austin Steil ("I", "me", or "my"). This policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit my website at austinsteil.com ("the Website").

I am committed to safeguarding your privacy and complying with applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and ePrivacy Directive. This policy outlines how I handle your data, including through the use of cookies for analytics, and informs you of your rights.

This Website is not intended for children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect their data.

By using my Website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions, please contact me using the details in the Contact Me section.

Information I Collect

I may collect the following types of information when you visit my Website:

  • Contact Information: If you contact me via email, I receive your email address and any information you choose to provide in your message.
  • Usage Data: Through Google Analytics (if you consent to analytics cookies), I collect data about your interactions with the Website, such as pages visited, time spent, bounce rates, and traffic sources.
  • Technical Data: This includes your IP address (anonymized for Google Analytics), browser type, device type, operating system, and approximate location (e.g., country or city, derived from IP address).
  • Cookies and Identifiers: Unique identifiers stored in cookies (e.g., `_ga` for Google Analytics) to track sessions and distinguish users, as detailed in the Cookies section.

How I Use Your Information

I use your information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries or messages sent to my email address.
  • To improve the Website's functionality and user experience.
  • To analyze usage trends and performance through Google Analytics (with your consent).
  • To ensure the security and integrity of the Website.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

My Website uses cookies to enhance functionality and analyze performance. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that may include a unique identifier. You can control cookie use through our consent banner.

Analytics Cookies: Used by Google Analytics to track user behavior anonymously and improve the Website. These are only set if you consent.

Cookie Consent

When you visit the Website, a cookie consent banner allows you to:

  • Accept All Cookies: Enables both essential and analytics cookies.
  • Accept Only Essential Cookies: Limits cookies to those necessary for Website functionality, disabling Google Analytics.

This ensures analytics cookies are only activated with your explicit consent, as required by privacy regulations.

Managing Cookies

You can update your cookie preferences anytime by clearing your browser cookies and reloading the page. Most browsers allow you to block cookies or alert you when they are set. Learn more at www.allaboutcookies.org.

Disabling cookies may affect some Website features. To opt out of Google Analytics entirely, install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

Third-Party Services

I use Google Analytics to monitor and analyze Website usage, but only if you consent to analytics cookies. Google Analytics is provided by Google, Inc. ("Google") and operates under its own privacy policies.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics uses cookies (e.g., `_ga` and `_ga_MVD9DZT628`) to collect data about your Website interactions, such as pages visited, session duration, and traffic sources. I have enabled IP anonymization, meaning the last octet of your IP address is masked (e.g., 192.168.12.144 becomes 192.168.12.0) to protect your privacy.

This data helps me understand user behavior and improve the Website. Google processes this data on servers that may be located in the United States, in compliance with the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework for data transfers. I have signed a Data Processing Agreement with Google on May 14th, 2025 to ensure GDPR compliance.

Google Analytics is only activated if you accept analytics cookies via the consent banner. If you deny consent, no analytics data is collected.

Google may share data with third parties if required by law or for processing on Google's behalf. For more details, visit How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or Google's Privacy Policy.

I implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights under privacy laws like GDPR or CCPA:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data.
  • Objection: Object to data processing for analytics or other purposes.
  • Portability: Request your data in a transferable format.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of analytics tracking via the Google Analytics Opt-Out Add-On or by denying cookies.

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Rights

Under the CCPA, California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is being collected about them
  • Know whether their personal information is sold or disclosed and to whom
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of their personal information
  • Access their personal information
  • Request deletion of their personal information

You can opt out of data sharing by selecting "Accept Only Necessary" in the cookie consent banner or by using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Add-On. You can also click the "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link in the footer to manage your preferences.

To exercise these rights, contact me at austinsteil21@gmail.com. I will respond within 30 days, as required by law.
