Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Austin Steil ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit my website at austinsteil.com ("the Website").

I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal data. This policy will inform you about how I look after your personal data when you visit my website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

When you visit my website, I may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: If you use the contact form, I collect your name, email address, and any message content you provide.
  • Usage Data: I may collect information about how you interact with my website, including pages visited, time spent on pages, and other browsing actions.
  • Technical Data: This includes your IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting, operating system, and platform.

How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect for the following purposes:

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

My website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on my website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amounts of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

Types of Cookies Used

This website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies help me understand how visitors interact with the website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps me improve the website based on user behavior.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit the website, you will see a cookie consent banner at the bottom of the page. You have the option to:

  • Accept All Cookies: This will allow all types of cookies, including analytics cookies.
  • Accept Only Necessary Cookies: This will only allow cookies that are essential for the website to function properly.

Managing Cookies

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and reloading the page. Additionally, most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Please note that if you choose to reject cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of my website.

Third-Party Services

I use third-party services to monitor and analyze the use of my website. These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies to help analyze how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States.

Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf.

Google Analytics is only loaded if you have accepted all cookies through the cookie consent banner. If you choose to accept only necessary cookies, Google Analytics will not be loaded and no data will be collected.

You can learn more about how Google uses your data at https://policies.google.com/privacy.
